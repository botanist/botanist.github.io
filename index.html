Collect

Our sensors measures your plants environment, for example soil moisture, temperature and light. We can also gather data from external sources such as weather sites.

Analyze

The collected data is processed, analyzed and compared with desired values. You can get a simple overview over deep details on your complete environment down to specific plants. All data is also saved historically for comparison with previous years.

Act

Create events from the analysis. If we think you are overwatering you can get a notification in your smartphone or if it's too cold in your greenhouse use our smart outlet to start a heater.

Welcome your plants to the digital age. They'll thank you.

Our smart assistant helps you succeed with your plants. We help you monitor and analyze their environment such as temperature, light and soil moisture. We'll give you advice on improvements that will increase your yields or prevent them from dying.

Wired prototype sensor

A complete solution. Our software and hardware working together.

Since we develop both the hardware in the form of sensors and actors as well as the software that controls it all we can achieve much higher level of integration between the two. Unpack, connect and start without the need of any programming skills.

You can of course integrate our system against others. Our API:s are open and use standard techniques such as JSON and HTTP.

Maximum flexibility. Wireless or wired.

Even if the future is wireless your wallet might not be quite there yet. For greenhouses were there's lots of plants in the same place we have wired sensors to keep the cost down. These connect to a hub that connects wirelessly to the rest of the system.

In order to preserve battery we use low-power radio. This limits the range to about 50 m outdoors. But as we can automatically route messages via devices we can reach around corners and other places where it normally doesn't reach.
