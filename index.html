Smart technology is not only for humans. Here at botani.st we're working hard to create a simple, open and affordable ecosystem for your plants. No matter if you only have a few plants or a whole garden you'll be a better cultivator with us.

Simplicity is key

Even if you are not tech-savy using our system is super easy. Plug in the control center, put some sensors in your plants and you are ready to go!1 Further configuration, if necessary, can be done via your smartphone/tablet or PC.

When botani.st senses there's something wrong with a plant, such as you forgot to water or it's too warm, it'll notify you and the sensor will flash red. When the problem is corrected the sensor will flash green again to indicate all is ok.

Via the smartphone/tablet or a PC you can get an overview of your installation as well as data for individual plants. Historical data is kept locally but a snapshot is available online2.

1. [botani.st doesn't require an internet connection to operate but one is recommended. It supports both ethernet and WiFi.]
2. [Requires internet access and registration.]

Growing your installation

Start small and grow as needed. All you is need the control center which also doubles as a hub and one or more sensors or actuators.

Our modular design lets you add hubs, sensors and actuators as you need them. We'll offer both cheap wired and a bit more expensive fully wireless sensors. Wired sensors and actuators connect to hubs which can handle up to 64 devices each3.

Hubs connect wirelessly to the control center and runs on either batteries or external power supply.

3. [Via the use of I/O expanders. Standard hub has 8 jacks.]

An open platform

Not only is your data yours but it is also there for you to access as you like. APIs using standard technologies such as REST and JSON lets you access all aspects of your sensor network. And if you like you can even build your own sensors and actuators and plug it into botani.st.

To improve our chances of doing good suggestions we'd like to to share your data and estimate how your plants feel. This will improve your and others chances of growing successs.
