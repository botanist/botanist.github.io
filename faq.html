Frequently Asked Questions

What is the range for the wireless devices?

In our tests we've got a good 50 m (150 ft) outdoors in direct-line-of-sight conditions and indoors about 20 m thru simple stud walls and brick walls.

That might sound a bit short but our system automatically routes traffic via intermediate devices if needed to reach its target. So in practice the short range is not a problem. This also lets use a less powerful radio which in turn consume less energy and increase battery life.

How far down is soil moisture measured?

Both the wireless and wired sensors have a 10 cm probe that is to be completely pushed into the soil. The probe is at the bottom of this so we measure between 5 cm and 10 cm down.

How many sensors do I need?

With artificial watering soil moisture is very local so to track that you'll need many. Natural light and temperature doesn't vary that much tho. However reading light via sensors under foliage can be tricky so we can aggregate light measurements from several sensors for a better and more accurate value.

Do the sensors come in different colors?

We haven't decided yet but probably yes. Let us know what colors you prefer!

How many devices can I have?

A lot more than you will probably ever need. The radio has a address space for ~65000 devices and them being all hubs with each having 64 sensors we're in the 4.2 million region. In practice the limit is likely to be less tho.

I have an android smartphone, will you support that?

It's the plan yes. At launch perhaps not.

Can I control irrigation with this?

Eventually yes! we're looking at interfacing with existing valve controls as well as providing our own.

When can I buy this?

Unfortunately not yet. We're hoping to begin public testing in a few months time and hopefully go into production in time for autumn.

What will it cost?

Our target is under 10 EUR for the basic wired sensor, a hub 25-30 EUR and likewise for the gateway. Altho the start cost may be a slightly larger than similar systems we believe that our hybrid wired/wireless solution is the right way for low cost-per-plant.
