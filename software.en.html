Software

Always available. When traveling and in the sofa

You can access your installation thru your browser or via an app in your smartphone. As all data is stored and analyzed on our machines it is accessible from wherever in the world. Excellent when you want to make sure the neighbor waters as promised.

A simple app. Always in your pocket

With our app for iPhone or Android you can see what needs to be taken care of, get a quick overview of your environment, detailed info on individual plants, make simple configuration, approve new join requests and much more.

With the camera you easily scan the device and get all details about it.

Database with advice. No more guessing

If you don't want to manually config each single value we have sensible defaults per plants in our database. It contains values for temperature, moisture and light. And if you plant seeds we can monitor each phase.

Each week you can rate your plants and thus help us improve our database.

Historic data. Analysis made easy

From the data we collect from the sensors not only do we calculate current values but also medians over hours, days and weeks. You can easily filter periods, see specific values, compare different sensors or run it against plants in our database to see if there would be a better match.

Data is presented both as charts and tables. If you want to do more complex analysis the data can easily be exported as CSV for use in MS Excel or other spreadsheet.
